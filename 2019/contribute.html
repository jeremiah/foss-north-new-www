Call for Papers

The Call for Papers is open until February 24 and the final speakers are contacted on March 10. We are happy to sponsor travel expenses and accomodation, but cannot pay for your time. Please visit the Call for Papers questionnaire to make your contribution.

The call for paper form consists of a title for the talk, an abstract, name and biography of the speaker, and preferred slot length (25 or 50 minutes, including Q&A). We also reemburse travel costs if needed. Since we have a budget to work with, we ask for the approximate cost that you want us to reemburse.

Thank you for contributing!

Call for Projects

Are you representing a project who is looking for a venue to do something? It can be a workshop, a development sprint, an install-fest or something completely different - you decide! If so, we would love for you to be a part of our community day which is held on April 7.

You bring the contents - we provide the venue. All the organizing participants gets tickets for the conference on April 8-9.

If you want to be part of this? Contact johan@foss-gbg.se for details.
