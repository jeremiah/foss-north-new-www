What?

foss-north is an free / open source conference covering both software and hardware from the technical perspective. Hosted in Gothenburg between Copenhagen, Oslo and Stockholm with an international airport, we provide a meeting place for the Nordic foss communities and will bring together great speakers with a great audience.
 
 

Videos

During the 2017 event all talks were recorded. You can find links to all the talks from the Speakers and Talks page.
 
 

Speakers

foss-north will provide a full day of interesting speakers. We are happy to announce that we have confirmed the following speakers.
 

Keynote Speakers

Lydia Pintscher
Lydia Pintscher has been contributing to free software for over 10 years in various projects. She studied computer science at KIT. Today she is the product manager of Wikidata, Wikimedia's knowledge base, as well as the president of KDE e.V.. She is the editor of Open Advise.
Knut Yrvin
Knut Yrvin is the co-founder of Skolelinux project which is now a part of Debian Edu. Many have heard about Knut through his work as Community Manager for Qt for Trolltech, Nokia and Digia. He started at Telenor, a phone carrier. He graduated with an engineering degree in electronics in 1992 and Masters degree in Computer Science and System Development in 2000. Knut has worked in various businesses from Telecom to consultancy and education.
Carl Josefsson
Carl Josefsson has more than ten years experience in the venture creation field. Today you might recognise him from the Startup Camp program at Chalmers Ventures. He has taking part in setting up and coaching several ventures and startups, spanning e.g. from medtech to online services. Before joining Chalmers Ventures he worked as investment manager and management for hire in the Gothenburg startup community. He holds a M. SC. in innovation and entrepreneurship from Chalmers School of Entrepreneurship.
