Contribute

A great conference is made by great contents. For great contents we need great sponsors. Please join us in making foss-north 2025 the best foss-north of them all by helping us bring together the best sponsors, contents and audience!

Call for Papers

We are currently accepting contributions for foss-north 2025!

The Call for Papers is open until Feb 1 and the final speakers are contacted no later than March 1. We are happy to sponsor travel expenses and accomodation, but cannot pay for your time. Please visit the Call for Papers registration page to make your contribution. This will take you to a login page. We use OAUTH2 for this and currently support GitHub and Google as ways to authenticate. Please contact us if you want us to add more providers.

The call for paper form consists of a title for the talk, an abstract, name and biography of the speaker, and preferred slot length (25 or 45 minutes, including Q&A). We also reimburse travel costs if needed. Since we have a budget to work with, we ask you to submit the approximate cost that you want us to reimburse.

Thank you for contributing!
