Announcing foss-north 2023

foss-north is a free / open source conference covering both software and hardware from the technical perspective. Hosted in Gothenburg between Copenhagen, Oslo, and Stockholm, with an international airport, we provide a meeting place for the Nordic foss communities and will bring together great speakers with a great audience.

Get your tickets at the tickets page.

The conference will be held on April 24 - 25 at Chalmers Conference Centre, Gothenburg, Sweden, paired with the community day on April 23. The event will be run on-site, in-person, in a physical venue.

The conference is held just after the members only LLW conference hosted by FSFE.

Call for Projects

The call for projects for the community day is open until April 15. For more information, visit the Contribute page.
