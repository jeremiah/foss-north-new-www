Call for Projects

Are you representing a project who is looking for a venue to do something? It can be a workshop, a development sprint, an install-fest or something completely different - you decide! If so, we would love for you to be a part of our community day which is held on April 23.

You bring the contents - we provide the venue. All the organizing participants gets a ticket discount for the conference on April 24 - 25.

Do you want to be part of this? Contact johan@foss-north.se for details.
