Announcing foss-north 2024

foss-north is a free / open source conference covering both software and hardware from the technical perspective. Hosted in Gothenburg between Copenhagen, Oslo, and Stockholm, with an international airport, we provide a meeting place for the Nordic foss communities and will bring together great speakers with a great audience.

Get your tickets at the tickets page.

The conference will be held on April 15 - 16 at Chalmers Conference Centre, Gothenburg, Sweden. The event will be run on-site, in-person, in a physical venue.

The conference is held just before the members only LLW conference hosted by FSFE.

Call for Papers

The call for papers is open until March 5. For more information, visit the Contribute page as well as our partner FSFE's CfP annoucement.
