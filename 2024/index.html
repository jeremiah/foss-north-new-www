Thanks for a great foss-north!

After two days of talks ranging all the way from how to use AI in FPGAs to frameworks for community decisions, foss-north 2024 is done! Thank you all for coming!

Videos and slides from the talks will be available shortly, see the schedule page. There are photos available as well.

 

foss-north is a free / open source conference covering both software and hardware from the technical perspective. Hosted in Gothenburg between Copenhagen, Oslo, and Stockholm, with an international airport, we provide a meeting place for the Nordic foss communities and will bring together great speakers with a great audience.

The conference was held on April 15 - 16 at Chalmers Conference Centre, Gothenburg, Sweden.

The conference was held just before the members only LLW conference hosted by FSFE.
